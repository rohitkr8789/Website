About Hotel Livya

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Established in 2010, Hotel Livya has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality in the heart of the city.

What began as a vision to create the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and exceptional service has evolved into one of the most prestigious hotels in the region. Our founder, Maria Livya, envisioned a place where guests would feel not just welcomed, but truly at home.

Over the years, we have hosted countless guests from around the world, each with their own unique stories and experiences. From business travelers seeking comfort during their work trips to families creating lasting memories, we have been privileged to be part of their journeys.
